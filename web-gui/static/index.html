Star System Generator for GURPS 4th Edition

Welcome!

This is a little tool to generate star systems for use in any story or Pen & Paper roleplaying game.
We use the rules from GURPS Space in the background and generate some stats that are of use in GURPS Space games.

The majority of the information is not game-specific, however, and is useful to anyone.

Options

You can select some options about the star system, or leave it completely random. The rules often generate hostile systems, as garden-type worlds are fairly rare. If you need to generate a system with such a world, it is preferable to force it. Note that even a garden-type world may be very cold or hot, as long as it falls in a range that humans can live in!
